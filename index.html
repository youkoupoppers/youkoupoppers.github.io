About the game

Planet Slinger is a one-button arcade game about nerve and timing. Your rocket climbs through space on its own. Tap to latch onto a planet's gravity, ride the orbit, and release at the perfect moment to slingshot toward the next planet. Hesitate and you drift. Mistime it and you crash. There's a bomb on your tail and only one rule: don't stop climbing.

Un jeu d'arcade à un bouton : catapulte ta fusée de planète en planète, accroche-toi aux orbites, et ne rate jamais le bon moment. Une erreur et c'est fini. Gratuit sur Google Play.

Features

  • One-button, one-life arcade tension
  • Satisfying orbital slingshot mechanic
  • A new planet every time — endless, procedurally generated
  • Handmade pixel art
  • Free to play — made entirely by one solo developer
